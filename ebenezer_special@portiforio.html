Ebenezer Special Home.

WELCOME

Ebenezer Special Home, also known as the
Kamembe's leading hotel, is a very popular hotel in Rusizi
District in Kamembe, Rwanda. As well as being the finest hotel
in the Rusizi, it is regarded as one of the most prestigious and
attractive public places in Rwanda. originally
known as Ebenezer Special Home, was established by
Pascal Ndagijimana in 2022. It was intended to
accomodate nobels who would attend meetings
taking place in Rusizi District by the time
of CHOGM . It took more customers, often
the children of nobels and otherdignitaries,
from both modern-day Rwanda and other foreigners.
